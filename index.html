INSTAX by FujiFilm

Instax is a brand of instant cameras and instant films made by Fujifilm. The Instax cameras are popular for their ability to print photos immediately after they are taken, similar to the classic Polaroid cameras. The Instax line includes various models, each with different features, styles, and sizes of prints.
